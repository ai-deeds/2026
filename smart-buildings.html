AI-DEEDS 2026 CTF Challenge

Smart Buildings Challenge

Design HVAC control agents for energy-efficient buildings

About the Challenge

Buildings account for approximately 40% of US carbon emissions. In this challenge, you will design HVAC control agents using Google's open-source building simulator to minimize carbon emissions and operating costs while maintaining comfortable building conditions.

Your solution could have massive real-world environmental impact. The winning team will be invited to give a talk at the AI-DEEDS 2026 workshop!

Getting Started

The challenge uses Google's open-source building simulator. Get started by cloning the repository and following the setup instructions.

How to Submit

  1. Develop your HVAC control agent using the simulator
  2. Write a short paper (up to 4 pages) describing your approach
  3. Submit your paper to the Smart Buildings track on HotCRP
  4. Include a link to your code repository in the submission

Prizes

Winner: Invited to give a talk at the AI-DEEDS 2026 workshop at ACM e-Energy!

Top submissions will also be considered for publication in the workshop proceedings.
